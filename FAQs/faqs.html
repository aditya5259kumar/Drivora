Will you arrange for a driver license for me?

Yes, we start processing your license right from the day we present you to DVLA for your learner's license.

Is it compulsory that I go to DVLA for the tests?

Yes, It is only DVLA that can issue a valid driving license for student drivers. therefore, the theory and practical tests.

WHAT IS THE DURATION OF THE PROGRAM?

The regular package lasts 6 weeks, the express lasts 3 weeks and polishing lasts 4 weeks.

DO I NEED TO GO THROUGH THE THEORY LESSONS?

Yes. It is mandated by DVLA that student drivers go through 12 topics on defensive driving before obtaining a learner's license.

DO YOU HAVE A SHORTER PERIOD OF COMPLETION?

The shortest completion period we have is 3 weeks and that is the express course.

I KNOW HOW TO DRIVE BUT I AM NOT VERY GOOD. HOW CAN YOU HELP?

You'll have to opt for the polishing/refreshers' program.

CAN I CHOOSE MY PREFERRED INSTRUCTOR?

Unfortunately no. Instructors are assigned to practical sessions based on their availability.

DO YOU DO THE ATOMIC AND TF PICK-UP FOR ALL SESSIONS?

Yes we do. We pick up and drop you off for every session.

DO YOU HAVE DISCOUNTS?

Yes we do. There are discounts for students and National Service Persons.
